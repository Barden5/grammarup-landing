FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.
Is GrammarUp free?
Yes, A1 and A2 levels are completely free forever. Upgrade to Premium for all 4 levels.
What age is GrammarUp for?
GrammarUp is designed for K-8 students ages 5-14 learning English as a second language.
Do I need to create an account?
You sign in with your existing Google account — no new password needed.
What is CEFR?
CEFR is the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages — an internationally recognised scale from A1 (beginner) to C2 (mastery). GrammarUp covers A1 to B2.
Can my child use it independently?
Yes! GrammarUp is fully self-directed. No teacher or parent needed to run a session.
How does the AI practice work?
After completing all topics in a level students unlock Free Practice and My Study Plan — both powered by AI that generates fresh lessons every time.
